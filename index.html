The 10 minute test

If you can't figure out how it works in 10 minutes put it down and run away.

The 10 minute test

  • Is there good documentation?
  • Is there training?
  • Are there examples or starter templates I can work with?
  • Can I pick this up and make it do something I want it to do in 10 min?

The Do's & Don'ts of Prototyping in Code

Don't forget about paper prototyping!

Don't run directly to code.
Pick the appropriate medium for your prototype.
Code has a production cost.

Manage expectations

Including your own

When presenting, always set expectations on the available functionality and the fidelity of the prototype.

Be reasonable with yourself regarding the level of fidelity and functionality required for a successful test.

Don't let the design or technology get away from you.

Digital tools can accelerate production but they can also pull you into trivial details.
